# Vision

## Product Purpose & Boundaries

### Purpose
Innovative Archive is a local printing business platform serving Ukhrul, Manipur. We empower local creators, artists, and startups by providing quality custom printing services for 3D prints, mugs, and t-shirts.

### Target Users
| Persona | Description | Primary Need |
|---------|-------------|--------------|
| Local Creator | Artists, designers wanting to sell custom merch | Easy product customization, fair pricing |
| Gift Buyer | People wanting personalized gifts | Quick ordering, quality products |
| Small Business | Startups needing branded items | Bulk ordering, consistent quality |
| Referrer | Users who want to earn commissions | Simple referral system, transparent payouts |

### Core Value Proposition
- **Local**: Based in Ukhrul, Manipur - supporting the local economy
- **Personal**: Run by 3 friends who care about quality
- **Simple**: WhatsApp-based ordering - no complex checkout
- **Rewarding**: Earn commission by referring friends

### Boundaries (Out of Scope)
- No online payment processing (WhatsApp-based for now)
- No shipping outside local region initially
- No real-time 3D product configurator in MVP
- No inventory management system

### Success Metrics
| Metric | Target | How to Measure |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| Monthly Orders | 50+ | Firestore orders collection |
| Referral Signups | 20% of users | Users with referredBy field |
| Repeat Customers | 30% | Orders per userId |
| Product Catalog | 20+ items | Firestore products collection |
